Solution Calculator

Examples / How to Use

For example: To make 100 mL of M/10 NaOH solution

  • Select Molarity
  • Enter 40 as Molecular Weight
  • Enter 0.1 for Concentration (M/10 = 0.1 M)
  • Enter 100 for Volume (in mL)

For example: To make 250 mL of N/10 H₂SO₄ from 35N stock

  • Select Normality
  • Enter 1 as Equivalence Factor
  • Enter 35 for Stock Concentration
  • Enter 0.1 for Required Concentration (N/10 = 0.1 N)
  • Enter 250 for Final Volume (in mL)
